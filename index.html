Context

Some important information to know beforehand, especially for dates or time estimates, is how it's measured. For cases where there's no year involved, referring to "Before the Tides" means before years were invented. Anything with a year is "After the Tides"

The Song is a force present within everyone, allowing them to use attunements, murmurs, and bells, all driving power from the song. (The song not being a literal song, just an entity or force)
