Context

Dates in Deep are pretty easy, considering there's no months. For cases where there's no year involved, referring to "Before the Tides" means before years were invented. Anything with a year is "After the Tides"

The Song is a force present within everyone, allowing them to use attunements, murmurs, and bells, all driving power from the song. (The song not being a literal song, just an entity or force)
