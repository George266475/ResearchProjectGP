Factions

Within Deep, 11 main factions are able to be interacted with. You can gain or lose reputation with each faction to gain benefits or be hunted down mercilessly. It's almost impossible for your reputation to not affect you, as towns and npcs will either try to kill you or praise you depending on your standing with them.




The Central Authority

The Central Authority is a military organization from the Central Luminant. Their goal is to conquer as much as they can while destroying those against them.

Etrea

Etrea is the main faction in the Etrean Luminant, controlling Etris and the Isle of Vigils. They are secretly ruled by a Ministry prophet, and are at war with the Authority.

Hive

Hive is a technologically advanced faction consisting of Vesperians, Ganymedes, and Chrysids. They use lifeweave to take the life out of their surrounding environment to power their own projects, such as giant trees known as Greathives.

The Authority


Originating from the Central Luminant, the Authority's reach to the game lies in Fort Merit, an Etrean prison taken over. They specialize in Flamecharm, Thundercall, and Frostdraw, each serving their own purpose in their ranks. Despite being peacekeepers, their ways of solving problems involves violence and backstabbing. Their military prowess is some of the best in Lumen, being a largely military organization, and having their higher-ups be outstanding warriors as well. The Authority seems to favor Adrets, as most of their ranks consist of solely Adrets, however footsoldiers can be any race.

  • Etrea. Initially at war to control the Eastern Luminant, however have changed after learning of The Lord Regent's control and origin as a Ministry Prophet. Their war would've been won because of the Authority's massive military, however transport between the Central Luminant and the Eastern/Etrean is impossible with the Eastern Lighthouse closed.

  • Hive. While not at war, frequently attack each other for unknown reasons. Most likely due to the Authority wanting to control the Eastern Luminant.

  • The Children of Navae

  • The Hundred Legions. After having broken a treaty between the Legions, the Authority finds themselves at war with them, however only the 11th Legion, a scouting force, is present in the Eastern Luminant.
  • Grand Sovereign of The Central Authority, Drallis Ehr, Overseer of Epochs.
